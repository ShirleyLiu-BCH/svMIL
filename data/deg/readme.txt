Folder containing files relevant to differentially expressed genes.

Sources:
BRCA.txt: Names of all differentially expressed genes across the BRCA TCGA cohort, generated with processExpressionData.R
BRCA_logFC.txt: Names and log fold change of all differentially expressed genes across the BRCA TCGA cohort, generated with processExpressionData.R
BRCA_zscores.txt: Names and z-scores of all differentially expressed genes across the BRCA TCGA cohort, generated with processExpressionData.R
